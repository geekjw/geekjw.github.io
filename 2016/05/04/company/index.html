Ace Corp and United Industries.
This is Ace Corporation(公司).
It was established in 1920, more than 90 years ago.
Ace manufactures(制造) sporting equipment such as tennis rackets(球拍), tennis balls, and fishing rods(鱼竿).
(Ace is a sporting goods manufacturer.)
It is a well-managed company, and its products are well made.
Its main office(head office) is in New York, and there is another office in Los Angeles.
(Its head office is in New York. and there is another office in L.A.)
ACE has two factories.
Its main factory is in Chicago. The other factory is in Miami.
Its head office in New York employs three hundred workers, and the L.A. Office has a staff(职员) of around fifty employees.
Five thousand people are employed in the Chicago factory, and another one thousand one hundred and fifty work in the Miami factory.
(Its head office has 300 employees. The L.A. office employs around 50)
Ace’s main customers are general consumers.
Its products are also sold to schools and other organizations.
In recent years the company has done well. Sales and profits are both increasing.
Its main competitors are foreign companies from Europe and Asia, Because of its high quality products, however, Ace has been successful.

United industries
This is United Industries, an American company.
(United Industries is an American company.)
It manufactures advanced products such as rocket engines(火箭发动机), satellites(卫星), and aircraft parts(飞机部件).
(Its main products are rocket engines, satellites and aircraft parts.)
A small group of engineers started the company in 1960.
It was established in 1960.
(United was established in 1960 by a small group of engineers)
At that time, it made rocket engines for other companies.
In 1965 it started to make aircraft parts, including small jet engines(喷射发动机).
Since then it has opened up new factories and offices in Europe and Asia.
United is based(基于) in Seattle, Washington, where its main factory is located.
(United’s head office and main factory is in seattle where it employs more than 15,000 workers)
It also has offices in Europe, Asia, and other locations around the world.
There are three factories, the main one is in Seattle, another is in Montreal, and the third is in London.
United employs between twenty and twenty-five thousand people.
(United is a large company, employing almost 25,000 people.)
Two thousand employees work in the London factory.
In Montreal, the company has five thousand employees, including part-time workers.
The two factories outside the US employ a total of around seven thousand workers.
In recent years the company has done well.
Sales and profits have both increased.
United sells its products to many different countries.
Some people don’t like United because some of its products are used for military(军人的) purposes.
Unfortunately, these kings of products are used too often.
