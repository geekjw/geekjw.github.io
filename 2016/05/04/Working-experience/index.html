Work Experience
Jean Nielsen
This is Jean Nielsen.
Jean graduated from Columbia University in 1996.
She majored in economics.
Two years later, at 1998, Jean was hired by Federal Motors.
(In 1983 she got a job with Federal Motors.)
Jean worked in Personnel(人事部) at Federal Motors for seven years, until 2005.
In 2005, she quit her job at Federal Motors and joined IBX.
She left FM in 2005 and got a job with IBX.
(In 2005 she quit her job at FB and joined IBX.)
Jean quit her job at FM because she wanted a more interesting job. IBX also hired her a higher salary.
At IBX she was hired as personnel manager in one of their factories.
As personnel manager, she has many responsibilities.
Jean is in charge of interviewing and hiring new employees.
She also managers the company’s employee training programs.
At her job she needs a good communication skills. Jean has excellent communication skills, so she has done very well at IBX.
Jean has worked in IBX since 2005.
Jean likes her job and is planning to stay in IBX for a long time.
